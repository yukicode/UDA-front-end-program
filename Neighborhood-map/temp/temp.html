Property Details

back to the top

Welcome to the relaxed elegance of The Palisades Apartment Community, which offers upscale living at an affordable price! We are in one of the best locations in Bellevue - just a few blocks away from Crossroads, the Microsoft Campus, and Downtown Bellevue area. There are so many shops and restaurants in the immediate area, you'll want to spend all your free time shopping and eating! Luckily, there's plenty of ways to burn those extra calories around the community, between our fitness center, outdoor pool, and basketball court. Our kitchens have been recently redesigned, so you can enjoy modern features like granite countertops and stainless steel appliances. We're close to Hwy. 520, 405 and I-90, giving you access to the rest of the Seattle area and Northwest region. Come see everything we have to offer at The Palisades and make an appointment today! The monthly rental rates provided are subject to change.

Leasing Terms

Lease terms are variable. Please inquire with property staff.
Pet Policy: Maximum 2 pets per apartment. Restrictions: $250.00 refundable deposit per pet & $250.00 Pet Fee per pet. $25.00 monthly pet rent per pet. We love all pets, but the following breeds/crossbreeds are not allowed: Pit Bulls (Staffordshire Bull Terrier), Dobermans, Rottweilers, Chow Chows, German Shepherds, Huskies, Mastiffs, Great Danes, Malamutes, Akitas and Wolf Hybrids. Breed restrictions are subject to change without notice.

Pet Policy

Dogs Allowed. Cats Allowed. Pets Accepted. Call for service animal policy.
